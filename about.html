Hello — I'm Mari.

This website is simply a place to write things down. Some thoughts are too small for essays but too interesting to disappear. Others take longer to form and benefit from having a quiet place to live while they grow.

Mari Thinks is not meant to be a blog or a publication. It’s closer to a notebook — a personal corner of the internet where ideas, observations, and unfinished thoughts can exist without pressure to perform or be optimized.

The site is intentionally simple: plain pages, minimal design, and no tracking or feeds. Just words and time to think.

If something here resonates with you, that’s wonderful. If it simply gives you a moment to pause and think, that’s just as valuable.
